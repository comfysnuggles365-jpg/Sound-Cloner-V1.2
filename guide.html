🔄 How Remix Works

  • Click 🎛 Remix on any song card
  • The remix panel shows the base style prompt (editable)
  • Pick a mood modifier (dark, euphoric, melancholic, etc.)
  • Pick a theme modifier (love, hustle, heartbreak, etc.)
  • Hit ⚡ Regenerate — the song is replaced with a fresh version using the new modifiers
  • Only THAT song changes — the rest of your batch stays intact
🎛 Also available: Click 🔄 Regen to quickly regenerate a single song with the same style (no remix panel).

6. Favourites

Star any artist or blueprint to save it to your Favourites tab for quick access. Your favourites persist across sessions.

⭐ How to Use Favourites

  • Click the ⭐ star on any artist or blueprint card to favourite it
  • Switch to the ★ Favourites tab to see all your saved items
  • Toggle between Artists and Blueprints views within Favourites
  • Unfavourite by clicking the star again on any card
  • Favourites are stored in your browser — no account needed
✅ Pro tip: Build a personal collection of go-to artists and blueprints for your signature sound. Star 10–15 diverse styles and mix between them for variety across releases.

🔬 How It Works

  • Switch to the 🔬 Analyse tab
  • Drag & drop an audio file (or click to browse)
  • Hit 🔬 Analyse Song
  • Gemini AI listens to the audio and outputs two prompts:
  • Voice Prompt — tone, texture, delivery, cadence, effects, mic style, era feel
  • Style Prompt — BPM, key, instruments, arrangement, genre blend, production texture, mix
  • 📋 Copy either prompt and paste into Suno or the Batch generator

💡 Why This Is Powerful

  • Swap voices onto different beats — copy a voice prompt from one song, pair it with a style prompt from another
  • Reverse-engineer why a track works — see the production DNA broken down
  • Clone your own voice — upload your own vocals to get a reusable voice prompt
  • 250 analyses/day free with a Gemini API key (no credit card needed)
💡 Requires a free Gemini API key. Get one at aistudio.google.com and paste it in the Analyse tab settings.
